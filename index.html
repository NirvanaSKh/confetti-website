Frequently asked questions

What exactly does the £150 fee cover?

It covers all of my time and work: planning the party, sourcing and booking suppliers, and coordinating everything up to a full handover before the day — a clear run-sheet, timings and supplier contacts so you can host with confidence. Supplier costs (venue, entertainer, catering, décor, etc.) are separate and charged to you at their actual cost, with no mark-up. You approve the full costings before anything is booked.

What ages and party sizes do you cover?

I organise parties for children roughly aged 1–11, from small gatherings at home to larger celebrations in a hall. Get in touch and I'll happily talk through what would work best for your family.

Which areas do you serve?

Sawbridgeworth and the surrounding area, including Bishop's Stortford, Harlow, Hertford, Ware and nearby villages. If you're just outside, please ask.

Are you and your suppliers insured?

Yes. I carry public liability insurance, and I only book suppliers who hold their own insurance and relevant safety certification.

How do I book, and what about deposits?

Start with a free, no-obligation chat. Once you're happy with the plan and costs, a deposit secures your date and the balance is due 1 week before the event. Full written terms are provided at booking, including a fair cancellation policy.

How far in advance should I book?

The earlier the better — popular halls and entertainers get booked up, especially at weekends and around school holidays. That said, do ask even for shorter notice; I'll always try to help.
